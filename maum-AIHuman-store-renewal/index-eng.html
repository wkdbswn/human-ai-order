Docent M1-Docent

It curates to explain art works and guide exhibition hall to visitors.

  • STT (speech recognition) & Touch Pad (Touch Pad)

  • STF (face reproduction) & TTS (voice generation)

    It provides vivid facial expressions and accents of a docent through STF and TTS.

  • 4K resolution

    With 4K resolution, it provides high quality resolution of AI human docent to a big-sized screen kiosk.

  • Multi-lingual TTS

    With Multi-lingual TTS, it provides a same tone of voice of docent in foreign languages, such as English, Japanese, and Chinese.

  • Device

    It can be applied to multiple devices such as smartphone, website, kiosk, digital signage, and robot.

  • Chatbot

    Besides the curation, it is capable of free talking with visitors through understanding their intentions from a question and a learning tool will be provided for manager to control continuous learning of the machine.

  • Usage

    Docent for exhibition hall, museum, and showroom. Guide at the home electronics shop.

Stenographer M1_Minutes

It precisely separates multiple speakers to record and share speeches of each person in a real-time.

  • Directional Microphone

    Directional microphone enables voice recognition and separation of speakers in a meeting.

  • CNN-STT engine

    With the application of ANN-STT engine, unstable long sentence also can be transferred into the text.

  • Audio Delete Level Function (ADL)

    Organically developed ADL function provides 86% of voice recognition without any additional learning process.

  • Real-time meeting recording

    It records individual speaker’s speech in a real-time.

  • Video file uploading function.

    It also can recognize voice from a video file to product texts.

  • All-in-One system

    Mic and the server are combined to be conveniently installed in any space.

  • Usage

    Produce meeting log for government organizations and firms, stenographic records at the court.
